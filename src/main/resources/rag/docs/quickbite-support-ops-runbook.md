# QuickBite — Support Ops Runbook (Synthetic, for RAG demos)

## Purpose

This runbook helps support agents handle common QuickBite customer issues consistently.
It complements the customer-facing policy KB and adds operational “how we do it” details.

## Standard Timelines

- Refund processing (card): 3–10 business days after approval
- Service credit: usually available immediately after approval
- Courier contact attempts: max two attempts over 10 minutes before escalation

## Evidence Guidelines (What to Ask For)

- Missing/wrong items: photo of items and bag label if present
- Spillage/damage: photo of packaging and affected items
- “Not delivered”: confirm address and phone, ask if someone else could have received it

## Late Delivery Severity Bands (Synthetic)

- Band A: 30–45 minutes late → recommend 10% service credit
- Band B: 46–75 minutes late → recommend 15–20% service credit
- Band C: 76+ minutes late → recommend 25% service credit or refund review

## Refund vs Credit Preference (Guideline)

Prefer REFUND when:

- Main item missing (entrée / primary dish)
- Dietary restriction makes the received order unusable
- Safety concerns are reported

Prefer CREDIT when:

- Minor add-on missing (sauce, topping)
- Slight delay but delivered

## Escalation Routing (Synthetic)

Escalate to Ops Review if:

- Order marked delivered but customer reports not received
- Repeated refund requests in the last 7 days
- Threats or chargeback intent is stated
